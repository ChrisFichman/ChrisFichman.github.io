Oh, I didn't see you there. Let me tell you about my highly diverse and intriguing set interests.

Where I'm From

I was born and raised in Columbia, MD, and went to River Hill High School. During high school I ran track and cross country, and won reigonals, and placed in the 2A State Championships. I also participated in several school theatre productions, and co-founded an a cappella group called the TalonTones.

Music

Music has always been my greatest passion in life. I've played piano since I was four, and I've been singing as long as I can remember. I've dabbled in guitar, and I'm currently teaching myself to play saxophone. I try to always have some element of music in my life, and currently I sing for Extreme Measures(and manage the website), an A Cappella group on campus, and last summer I did vocal procussion for a professional group, 5280 A Cappella.

Noteflight - A link to the site on which I arrange music.

Other Interests

Long walks on the beach, ALL of the sports, Astrophysics(Helioseismology), Neuroscience, Movies, Indian Food, Creative Writing and Video Games.
